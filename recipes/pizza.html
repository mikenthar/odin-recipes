Mom's Best Pizza

Description

This is my mom's favorite pizza recipe. We grew up in a small town south of Naples, Italy where this was part of the local faire. I can still remember coming home from a long day working in the vineyards with my father and smelling the tantalizing aromas of a delicious, home cooked, italian meal. I hope that this recipe is equally enjoyable for you and your family.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pizza Crust
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions

  1. Mix together all the wet ingredients
  2. Mix together all the dry ingredients
  3. Mix together the wet and dry ingredients
  4. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees
  5. Bake lasagna at 450 degrees for 30 minutes
  6. Remove from heat, let stand for 10 minutes and serve
